SpotMe CUSEC

Find yourself in CUSEC 2026 photos

How to use

  1. Upload a clear photo of your face (selfie works best)
  2. Click "Spot Me" to search through event photos
  3. Click any result to view full size

Settings

Threshold (0.0 - 1.0)

How similar a face must be to match. Lower = more results but less accurate. Higher = fewer but more accurate matches. Default 0.4 works well.

Max Results

Maximum number of photos to return. Increase if you want to see more matches.

* SpotMe does not save or store any images you upload. All photos shown are from publicly available sources only.
